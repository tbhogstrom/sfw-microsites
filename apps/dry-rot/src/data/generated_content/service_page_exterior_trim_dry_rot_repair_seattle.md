## Service Overview

### What is Exterior Trim Dry Rot Repair?

Exterior trim dry rot repair involves assessing and addressing the damage caused by moisture and decay. This process typically includes the removal of rotten sections, reinforcement of the remaining trim using high-strength materials or epoxy, and the installation of new trim as necessary. According to industry best practices, a surgical approach to cutting back to solid wood and using appropriate adhesives ensures a lasting repair [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 173]. Our team utilizes advanced techniques to ensure that the repairs are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing [Source: Christina Glennon, Siding, Roofing, and Trim, Page 197].
