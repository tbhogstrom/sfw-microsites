## Service Overview

### What is Seasonal Dry Rot Assessment?

A Seasonal Dry Rot Assessment involves a comprehensive inspection of your home's exterior, focusing on areas where wood is most susceptible to moisture and decay. Our trained professionals utilize specialized tools and techniques to identify signs of dry rot, including visual inspections and moisture readings. By addressing these areas, we help prevent the further deterioration of wood components, which can lead to significant structural issues over time.

During our assessment, we examine exterior trim, siding, windows, and other areas prone to water exposure. We also check for common indicators of rot, such as discoloration, soft spots, and fungal growth.
