## Service Overview

### What is Rotten Window Sill Repair?

Rotten window sill repair involves the assessment, removal, and replacement of damaged wood in and around your window sills. The process begins with identifying the extent of the rot and whether it is localized or part of a larger issue. Using techniques outlined in leading construction handbooks, we ensure that the repair is thorough and effective.

Repair involves:
1. **Removing Rot:** Cut away damaged wood to expose solid material.
2. **Reinforcing Structure:** If necessary, install new supports or flashing to prevent future moisture intrusion [Source: Renovation, Page 140].
3. **Replacing or Repairing Sills:** Utilize rot-resistant materials to fabricate new sills or repair existing ones [Source: Renovation, Page 156].
