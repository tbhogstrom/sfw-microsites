## Service Overview

### What is Emergency Dry Rot Repair?

Emergency dry rot repair involves the identification, assessment, and remediation of wood decay caused by fungal growth, primarily affecting structural elements of your home. This service encompasses several critical steps, including the removal of affected wood, treating the area with epoxy or wood hardener, and replacing damaged sections with durable materials to prevent recurrence [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 196].

When addressing dry rot, it is crucial to address the underlying moisture issues that contribute to wood decay. This might involve sealing leaks, improving ventilation, or utilizing moisture barriers to protect susceptible areas.
