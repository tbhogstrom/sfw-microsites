## Service Overview

### What is Rotten Trim Scarf Joint Repair?

Rotten trim scarf joint repair is a technique used to address deteriorating sections of exterior trim without the need for complete replacement. This process involves cutting back the damaged trim at a 45-degree angle to create a scarf joint, allowing for the secure attachment of a new piece of trim. We utilize high-strength epoxy adhesives to ensure a durable bond that withstands Seattle’s weather conditions. This method is particularly effective for trims made from old-growth wood, which can be challenging to replace due to its unique characteristics and historical value [Source: Glennon, Page 196].
