## Service Overview

### What is Comprehensive Dry Rot Assessments?

Comprehensive Dry Rot Assessments involve a meticulous examination of your home's wooden structures, including trim, siding, and frames. Our skilled technicians will look for signs of rot, moisture intrusion, and insect damage, employing tools such as moisture meters and thermal imaging cameras for thorough detection. We will also assess any visible damage, including crumbling wood or discoloration, and determine the extent of the decay. This process is crucial in addressing the root cause of rot, which may include poor drainage, plumbing leaks, or inadequate ventilation [Source: Litchfield, Page 71].
