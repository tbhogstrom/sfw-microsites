## Service Overview

### What is Porch Column Dry Rot Repair?

Porch column dry rot repair involves the assessment and restoration of wooden columns that have been compromised by dry rot. This process typically includes:

1. **Assessment**: Evaluating the extent of rot damage.
2. **Removal**: Removing any severely compromised wood.
3. **Repair Techniques**: Utilizing high-strength epoxy and consolidation methods on less damaged areas to reinforce the wood [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 173].
4. **Replacement**: In cases where extensive rot is found, replacing the affected columns with new, durable materials is often necessary [Source: Christina Glennon, Siding, Roofing, and Trim, Page 196].
