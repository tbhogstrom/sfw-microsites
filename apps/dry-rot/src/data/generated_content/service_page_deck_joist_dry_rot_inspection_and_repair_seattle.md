## Service Overview

### What is Deck Joist Dry Rot Inspection and Repair?

Deck joist dry rot inspection and repair involves a thorough examination of the wooden components of your deck, particularly the joists that support its structure. During the inspection, our experts will look for signs of dry rot, which is caused by certain fungi that thrive in damp conditions. This includes checking for soft, crumbly wood, discoloration, and any signs of insect damage, which can exacerbate the issue [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 71].
