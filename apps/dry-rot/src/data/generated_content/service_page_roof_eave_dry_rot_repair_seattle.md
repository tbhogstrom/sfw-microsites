## Service Overview

### What is Roof Eave Dry Rot Repair?

Roof eave dry rot repair involves assessing and treating areas of your roof's eaves affected by dry rot. This process typically includes:

1. **Inspection**: A thorough examination of the eaves to identify areas affected by rot.
2. **Removal**: Carefully removing damaged wood while minimizing disruption to surrounding structures. We use techniques like cutting back to undamaged wood and applying epoxy repairs when necessary [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 173].
3. **Replacement**: Installing new, rot-resistant materials that meet local building codes.
4. **Sealing and Finishing**: Properly sealing the new materials to prevent future moisture issues.

Our team adheres strictly to industry standards and follows recommended installation practices to ensure a long-lasting solution.
