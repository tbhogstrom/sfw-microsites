## Service Overview

### What is Window Sill Dry Rot Replacement?

Window sill dry rot replacement involves the removal of compromised window sills that have been damaged by moisture and fungal growth. During this process, our experts assess the extent of the damage, replace the affected wood with high-quality materials, and ensure proper sealing to prevent future rot. For effective replacement, we often utilize flexible, self-adhesive flashing tape to construct a watertight barrier, which is critical for longevity in a rainy area like Seattle [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 140].
