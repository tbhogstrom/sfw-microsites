## Service Overview

### What is Bathroom Restoration and Remodeling?

Bathroom restoration and remodeling involve updating and renovating your existing bathroom to improve its functionality and appeal. This process may include tearing out old fixtures, updating plumbing and electrical systems, installing new tile or flooring, and enhancing storage solutions. According to Michael Litchfield in his comprehensive guide on renovation, the flow from demolition to construction is vital, requiring careful planning to ensure that adjacent areas remain intact during the renovation process [Source: Litchfield, Page 207].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's unique climate, with its wet winters and mild summers, can lead to specific bathroom issues such as mold growth and water damage. Additionally, older homes may not meet current building codes, making renovations essential for safety and functionality. Updating your bathroom not only enhances your living experience but also ensures compliance with local regulations, helping you avoid potential fines or safety hazards.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Outdated Fixtures**: Old plumbing fixtures can lead to leaks and inefficiencies [Source: Litchfield, Page 39].
- **Poor Layout**: Many bathrooms are poorly designed, making them feel cramped and uncomfortable [Source: Litchfield, Page 31].
- **Water Damage**: Renovating can help address issues like mold and rot, particularly in older homes [Source: Litchfield, Page 8].
- **Lack of Storage**: Many bathrooms lack adequate storage, which can be resolved through custom cabinetry and shelving [Source: Litchfield, Page 32].
