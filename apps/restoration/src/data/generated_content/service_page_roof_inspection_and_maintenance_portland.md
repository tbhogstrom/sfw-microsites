## Service Overview

### What is Roof Inspection and Maintenance?

Roof inspection and maintenance involve a systematic examination of your roofing system to identify any signs of wear, damage, or potential failure. This process includes checking for common issues such as sagging rafters, missing or damaged shingles, deteriorated flashing, and inadequate drainage systems [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 19]. Our inspections typically take two to three hours and yield a detailed report outlining any necessary repairs and maintenance recommendations.

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's unique climate, characterized by heavy rainfall and seasonal weather fluctuations, creates specific challenges for homeowners. Regular roof inspections are vital to ensure that your home can withstand these elements without suffering from water damage or structural issues. Additionally, local building codes often mandate certain standards for roofing systems, making professional inspections essential for compliance [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 18].

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Water Damage**: Without proper maintenance, roofs can develop leaks that lead to extensive water damage and mold growth [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 19].
- **Structural Integrity Issues**: Inspections can reveal sagging rafters or undersized support beams, which could compromise the entire roofing structure [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 18].
- **Flashing Deterioration**: Missing or rusted flashing can allow water to penetrate and damage the roof and interior of your home [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 19].
- **Gutter Problems**: Clogged or absent gutters can exacerbate drainage issues, leading to potential foundation problems and increased repair costs [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 18].
