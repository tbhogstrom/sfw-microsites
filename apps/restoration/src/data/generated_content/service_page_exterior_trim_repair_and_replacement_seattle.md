## Service Overview

### What is Exterior Trim Repair and Replacement?

Exterior trim repair and replacement involve assessing, fixing, or replacing sections of trim around windows, doors, eaves, and other exterior areas of your home. This work includes identifying signs of rot, water damage, or deterioration, and making necessary repairs to restore or replace trim boards with high-quality, rot-resistant materials. 

Common techniques include using marine epoxy for permanent repairs to rotten sections [Source: Glennon, Page 197] and ensuring that new trim is installed correctly to minimize future issues.
