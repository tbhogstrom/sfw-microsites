## Service Overview

### What is Aluminum Siding Repair?

Aluminum siding repair involves assessing and fixing any damage to the exterior aluminum panels of your home. This can include replacing panels, repairing dents, repainting, and sealing gaps to prevent water intrusion. Our technicians use high-quality materials and techniques to ensure a long-lasting solution, adhering to best practices outlined in construction handbooks, including using rust-resistant fasteners to secure the siding properly [Source: Charlie Wing, Page 252].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington

In Seattle, the moist climate can contribute to corrosion and wear on aluminum siding. Moreover, Seattle's building codes may require homeowners to maintain their siding to meet safety standards, particularly in areas prone to heavy rainfall.
