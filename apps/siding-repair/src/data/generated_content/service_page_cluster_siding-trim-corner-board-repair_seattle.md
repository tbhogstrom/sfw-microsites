## Siding Trim Repair
Siding trim repair is essential for maintaining the structural integrity and appearance of your home, especially in Seattle's damp climate. Over time, exposure to moisture can cause wood trim to rot, warp, or crack, which not only detracts from your home's aesthetic but can also lead to more significant issues like water infiltration. Rot-resistant woods such as cedar or redwood are recommended for exterior trim because they can withstand the Pacific Northwest's moisture levels better than other types of wood<sup>1</sup>. Additionally, it's crucial to select stainless steel or hot-dipped galvanized nails for installation to prevent rust and ensure long-term durability<sup>2</sup>. Regular maintenance, such as repainting or sealing, is also vital to protect the trim from moisture and UV damage. If you suspect your siding trim needs repair, act promptly to prevent more extensive damage to your home's exterior. Contact our team to schedule an inspection and protect your home from Seattle's challenging weather conditions.

## Corner Board Repair
Corner boards are essential for protecting and enhancing the visual appeal of your home's exterior, especially in the Pacific Northwest where rain and moisture are prevalent. These boards, typically 1-inch thick, serve as a junction for siding, providing a seamless and weatherproof transition between walls<sup>3</sup>. Over time, corner boards can suffer from moisture penetration, leading to rot and structural damage. Repairing corner boards involves preassembling them before installation to ensure a crisp, straight corner<sup>4</sup>. This process includes priming and painting to safeguard against the elements. When damaged, boards should be carefully removed, and new ones installed using rust-resistant nails, ensuring a tight fit against the siding. It's crucial to predrill nails to prevent splitting, and use liberal caulking to seal gaps and prevent water intrusion. If dealing with inside corners, simpler 1-inch by 1-inch strips can be used as they are less exposed to weather<sup>3</sup>. Addressing corner board issues promptly can prevent further damage and maintain your home's exterior integrity. If your Seattle home's corner boards need attention, schedule a detailed inspection and repair today to protect your investment.
