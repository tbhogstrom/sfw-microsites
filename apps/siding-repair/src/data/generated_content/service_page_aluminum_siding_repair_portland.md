## Service Overview

### What is Aluminum Siding Repair?

Aluminum siding repair involves assessing and fixing issues related to your aluminum exterior cladding. Common repairs include replacing damaged panels, sealing joints, and repainting to restore the original look. During the repair process, we ensure that all joints are properly sealed to prevent moisture intrusion, which can lead to further complications. Our technicians are trained to use rust-resistant fasteners and appropriate sealing materials to maintain the integrity of the siding [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 252].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland, the climate varies significantly, with wet winters and dry summers. This means aluminum siding is exposed to moisture and humidity, which can lead to corrosion and other damage if not properly maintained. Furthermore, adhering to local building codes and ensuring your siding is in good condition can prevent expensive repairs in the future. Keeping your siding in top shape also contributes to the overall value of your home, making it essential for homeowners in our area.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Dents and Scratches**: These can compromise the aesthetic and structural integrity of your siding.
- **Corrosion**: Exposure to moisture can lead to rust, particularly at the joints and fasteners [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 252].
- **Loose Panels**: Over time, fasteners may loosen, leading to flapping or detachment of siding panels [Source: Christina Glennon, Siding, Roofing, and Trim, Page 44].
