## Service Overview

### What is Siding Installation for New Construction?

Siding installation for new construction involves applying exterior cladding materials to the exterior walls of a newly built home. This comprehensive service includes selecting the right type of siding—be it fiber-cement, wood, vinyl, or other materials—preparing the surface, and ensuring that the installation adheres to the highest industry standards. Key steps include:

- **Installing a Starter Strip**: A level starter strip is essential for the first course, allowing for proper alignment and drainage [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 244].
- **Nailing and Overlap**: Each course of siding must be properly nailed, ensuring that joints overlap appropriately to minimize water infiltration [Source: Charlie Wing, Page 244].
- **Moisture Barrier**: Installing a water-resistant barrier is crucial to protect against moisture damage, especially in Portland’s rainy climate [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 426].
