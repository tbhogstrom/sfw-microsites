## Service Overview

### What is Vinyl Siding Replacement?

Vinyl siding replacement involves removing old or damaged vinyl panels and installing new ones to restore the exterior of your home. The process is straightforward: we first assess the condition of your current siding, remove the damaged panels, and replace them with new, high-quality vinyl siding. Each panel is designed to interlock, ensuring a seamless look while allowing for necessary movement due to temperature fluctuations [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 49].
