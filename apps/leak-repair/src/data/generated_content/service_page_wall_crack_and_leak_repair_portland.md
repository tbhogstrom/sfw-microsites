## Service Overview

### What is Wall Crack and Leak Repair?

Wall crack and leak repair entails identifying, assessing, and rectifying cracks in walls and leaks that may stem from various sources such as water intrusion, foundation shifts, or settling. Our process involves:

1. **Assessment**: We inspect the extent and cause of wall damage.
2. **Preparation**: Selecting appropriate materials and methods, including hydraulic cement for foundational cracks, epoxy solutions for minor fissures, and plaster for wall repairs [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 247].
3. **Execution**: Employing techniques such as undercutting cracks for better adhesion and using commercial-grade sealants to ensure durability [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 246].
4. **Finishing Touches**: A quality check to smooth out any repairs and ensure seamless integration with existing structures.
