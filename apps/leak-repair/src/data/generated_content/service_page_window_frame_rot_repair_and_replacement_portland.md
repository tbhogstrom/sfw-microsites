## Service Overview

### What is Window Frame Rot Repair and Replacement?

Window frame rot repair and replacement involves identifying and fixing rot in window frames, which can occur due to water infiltration, poor maintenance, or age. The process often begins with an initial assessment to determine the extent of the damage. If the rot is extensive, we may recommend replacing the entire window frame for a more long-lasting solution. This includes removing the damaged sections and replacing them with new, rot-resistant materials while ensuring proper sealing to prevent future issues [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 152].
