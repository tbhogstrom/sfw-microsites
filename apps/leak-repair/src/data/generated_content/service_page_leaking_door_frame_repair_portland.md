## Service Overview

### What is Leaking Door Frame Repair?

Leaking door frame repair involves identifying and fixing issues that allow water and air to penetrate the frame surrounding your doors. This service includes inspecting the frame for rot, gaps, or damage, and applying the necessary repairs, such as replacing damaged materials, applying weatherstripping, and ensuring a proper seal around the door frame using flashing techniques. 

To effectively seal the frame, we may also install insulation and use specialized materials that help prevent future leaks, ensuring that your home remains protected from the elements [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 139].

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Water Damage to the Door Frame**: Excessive moisture can cause the wood to rot, leading to deterioration that requires replacement [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 139].
- **Drafts Around the Door**: Air leaks can make your home uncomfortable and increase energy bills; sealing these gaps is crucial [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 139].
- **Improperly Installed Flashing**: If flashing is poorly installed, water can collect around the door frame, leading to leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 139].
- **Structural Integrity Issues**: A warped door frame can cause misalignment, making it difficult to open or close the door properly [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 130].
