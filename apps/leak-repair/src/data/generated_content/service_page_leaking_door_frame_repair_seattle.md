## Service Overview

### What is Leaking Door Frame Repair?

Leaking door frame repair involves diagnosing and fixing leaks that occur around door frames, which can compromise the integrity of your home. This service typically includes assessing the door frame for signs of damage or wear, sealing gaps, replacing deteriorated materials, and installing new weatherstripping or flashing to prevent future leaks. 

As noted in construction best practices, it's crucial to inspect door frames for rot and ensure that jambs are plumb and square to avoid further complications [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 150]. Proper installation and maintenance of door frames are essential to prevent water ingress, which can lead to mold growth and structural damage over time.

Seattle's climate, characterized by high rainfall and humidity, makes it imperative for homeowners to address door frame leaks swiftly. The consistent moisture can exacerbate wood rot and compromise the structural integrity of your home.
