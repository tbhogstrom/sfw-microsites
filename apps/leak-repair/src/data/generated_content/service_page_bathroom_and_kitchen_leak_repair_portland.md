## Service Overview

### What is Bathroom and Kitchen Leak Repair?

Bathroom and kitchen leak repair involves identifying and fixing leaks in plumbing systems, fixtures, and appliances. Common sources of leaks include worn-out wax gaskets beneath toilets, corroded supply lines, and improperly sealed fixtures [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 26]. Our technicians utilize advanced tools and techniques to locate leaks, assess the extent of damage, and implement effective repairs.

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland’s humid climate, water damage can escalate quickly due to consistent rainfall and moist conditions. Homes built in older neighborhoods may also face unique plumbing challenges, making proactive leak repair crucial. By addressing leaks promptly, homeowners can avoid major repairs and ensure their homes comply with local building codes.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Worn Wax Ring Gaskets**: A common cause of leaks at the base of toilets, which can lead to significant subfloor damage [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 471].
- **Corroded Supply Lines**: Metal supply lines can corrode over time, leading to leaks that may not be immediately visible [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 393].
- **Improperly Sealed Fixtures**: Poor sealing around sinks and bathtubs can allow water to seep into walls, causing hidden damage [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 26].
- **Damaged Drainpipes**: Old or damaged drainpipes can lead to leaks that not only waste water but also create hazardous living conditions [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 471].
