- **Faulty Installation**: Many roof leaks stem from poor installation practices, such as misaligned starter courses or improperly installed flashing [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 188].
- **Material Defects**: Certain roofing materials, like shingles with continuous seal-down strips, can trap water and lead to leaks [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 190].
