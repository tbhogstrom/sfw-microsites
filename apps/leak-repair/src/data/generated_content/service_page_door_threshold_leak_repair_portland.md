## Service Overview

### What is Door Threshold Leak Repair?

Door threshold leak repair involves assessing and fixing any gaps or damages in the door threshold that allow water to seep into your home. This service includes the installation of a durable three-piece threshold system featuring a sill cover, drain pan, and compression gasket. Proper installation ensures that the threshold directs water away from the door and prevents moisture intrusion [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 136].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland’s climate is characterized by significant rainfall, making effective door threshold sealing crucial for preventing water damage and maintaining energy efficiency. A well-functioning door threshold protects against wind-driven rain and prevents the onset of mold and rot, which can occur when moisture is allowed to enter [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 137]. 

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Water Intrusion**: Poorly sealed or damaged thresholds lead to water leaking into the home, which can cause structural damage and mold growth [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 136].
- **Drafts and Energy Loss**: Ineffective thresholds allow drafts to enter, leading to higher heating and cooling costs [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 135].
- **Door Misalignment**: A damaged threshold can affect door operation, causing it to stick or misalign [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 137].
- **Aesthetic Issues**: Weathered or damaged thresholds detract from your home’s curb appeal [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 138].
