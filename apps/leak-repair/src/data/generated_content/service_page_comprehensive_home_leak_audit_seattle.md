## Service Overview

### What is Comprehensive Home Leak Audit?

A comprehensive home leak audit is a systematic assessment of your home to identify areas where conditioned air escapes and outside air infiltrates. This audit typically includes:

- **Visual Inspection**: Our team will visually assess potential leak sites such as windows, doors, and electrical outlets.
- **Blower Door Test**: This diagnostic test measures the air tightness of your home and helps pinpoint leaks by creating a controlled pressure difference [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 401].
- **Thermal Imaging**: We utilize thermal imaging cameras to detect temperature variations that indicate air leaks.
- **Smoke Testing**: This technique helps visualize airflow patterns and identify leaks in ducts and other areas [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 398].
