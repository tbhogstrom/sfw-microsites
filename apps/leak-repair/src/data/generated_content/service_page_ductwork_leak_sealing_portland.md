## Service Overview

### What is Ductwork Leak Sealing?

Ductwork leak sealing involves the identification and repair of leaks within your home's duct system. Leaks can occur due to various factors, including improper installation, wear and tear, or damage from pests. Our process typically begins with a thorough assessment of your ductwork, often employing techniques such as pressurization tests and smoke tests to locate leaks. Once identified, we utilize high-quality materials like fiber-reinforced mastic sealant to effectively seal the leaks and ensure an airtight duct system [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 406].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland, where temperatures can fluctuate significantly throughout the year, maintaining a consistent indoor climate is essential. Poorly sealed ducts can lead to increased energy consumption and higher utility bills, especially during peak heating and cooling seasons. Additionally, with the potential for mold and mildew growth in damp conditions, sealing your ductwork can improve indoor air quality, making your home healthier and more comfortable [Source: Charlie Wing - The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 479].
