## Service Overview

### What is Exterior Leak Detection and Sealing?

Exterior leak detection and sealing is a comprehensive service that involves identifying and sealing leaks in the external envelope of your home. This includes inspecting and addressing air leaks around windows, doors, and vents, as well as potential leaks in the roof and foundation areas. Utilizing techniques such as pressurization and thermal imaging, our team can effectively pinpoint leaks that may not be visible to the naked eye. Once identified, we use high-quality sealants, weather stripping, and other materials to ensure these leaks are effectively sealed, preventing air and water infiltration [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 479].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington

Seattle's climate presents unique challenges for homeowners. With frequent rain and varying temperatures, moisture intrusion can lead to serious issues such as rot, mold, and structural damage. Additionally, air leaks can significantly impact your home's energy efficiency, particularly in older houses where building materials may degrade over time. By investing in exterior leak detection and sealing, homeowners can safeguard their properties against these challenges while ensuring compliance with local building codes.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Air Leakage**: Uncontrolled air exchange leads to energy loss, which can be effectively managed through sealing techniques [Source: Charlie Wing, Page 479].
- **Moisture Intrusion**: Water leaks can cause damage to walls and foundations, leading to costly repairs [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 122].
- **Mold Growth**: Excess moisture can lead to harmful mold, which poses health risks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 399].
- **Energy Waste**: Leaky ducts in forced air systems can waste energy and create negative pressure, pulling harmful gases back into living spaces [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 399].
