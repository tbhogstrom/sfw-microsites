## Service Overview

### What is Siding and Window Integration Repair?

Siding and window integration repair involves the systematic assessment and rectification of the joints where windows meet the siding of your home. This service typically includes the replacement of deteriorating window frames, re-caulking gaps, and applying flashing to prevent water ingress, which is crucial in the Pacific Northwest's wet climate. Techniques such as using self-adhesive flashing tape and rigid perimeter flashing ensure that the window installation is weather-tight, thereby reducing air infiltration and enhancing energy efficiency [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 147].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland, the climate is characterized by significant rainfall and humidity. These conditions make homes particularly susceptible to mold growth and wood rot if siding and window integration are not properly maintained. Building codes in Oregon mandate strict adherence to weatherproofing standards, making it vital for homeowners to ensure that their siding and windows are integrated effectively to avoid costly future repairs.
