## Service Overview

### What is Bay Window Leak Repair?

Bay window leak repair involves a comprehensive assessment and repair process to address and rectify leaks that may be entering your home through the window structure. This service includes:

1. **Inspection**: Identifying the source of the leak, which can arise from faulty installation, damaged flashing, deteriorated seals, or even rotting wood [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 140].
2. **Repair Techniques**: Utilizing advanced methods such as applying self-adhesive flashing tape, resealing the glass with high-quality glazing compounds, and replacing damaged wood as necessary [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 156].
3. **Weatherproofing**: Ensuring that your bay windows are properly sealed and insulated to prevent future leaks and air infiltration [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 141].

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Deteriorated Seals**: Over time, seals can wear down, allowing water to seep in. This is often linked to poor maintenance practices [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 154].
- **Faulty Installation**: Improper installation can lead to gaps and misalignment, causing leaks and drafts [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 140].
- **Rotting Wood**: Wood rot in the window frame can compromise the integrity of the structure and lead to further water damage if not addressed swiftly [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 156].
- **Insufficient Flashing**: Without adequate flashing, water can accumulate and enter the home, necessitating professional intervention for repairs [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 141].
