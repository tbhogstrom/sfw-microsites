## Service Overview

### What is Window Frame Rot Repair and Replacement?

Window frame rot repair and replacement involves assessing and addressing decayed wood within window frames, often caused by moisture and poor drainage. The process typically includes inspecting the frame for rot, removing affected areas, and either replacing them with new wood or using epoxy putties for minor repairs [Source: Renovation, Page 152]. For severe cases, a complete window unit replacement may be necessary, ensuring that the new installation is properly sealed and insulated to prevent future issues [Source: Renovation, Page 150].

Seattle's unique climate—with its frequent rain and humidity—makes homes particularly susceptible to window frame rot.
