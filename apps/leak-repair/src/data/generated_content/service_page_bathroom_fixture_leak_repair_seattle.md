### What is Bathroom Fixture Leak Repair?

Bathroom fixture leak repair involves identifying, diagnosing, and fixing leaks from various fixtures in your bathroom, including faucets, toilets, and showerheads. Common issues include leaks at the tank-to-bowl juncture of toilets, which can often be fixed by tightening the nuts, or replacing a failed wax ring if leakage occurs at the base [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 360].
