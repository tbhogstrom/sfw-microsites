## Service Overview

### What is Wall Crack and Leak Repair?

Wall crack and leak repair involves identifying and fixing cracks in walls and leaks that penetrate the building envelope. This service encompasses a detailed assessment of the damage, followed by the application of specialized repair techniques and materials. Common methods include using hydraulic cement for filling cracks, applying epoxy for waterproofing, and reattaching plaster to lath if necessary [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 246].
