## Service Overview

### What is Faucet and Fixture Leak Repair?

Faucet and fixture leak repair involves identifying, diagnosing, and rectifying leaks in various plumbing fixtures, including sinks, faucets, and showers. Our technicians perform a thorough assessment to determine the source of the leak, which could be due to worn-out seals, corrosion, or improper installation. For instance, faucets made from brass are less likely to leak because they resist corrosion and maintain tight tolerances [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 393].

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Worn Seals**: Over time, seals in faucets can wear out, causing leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 387].
- **Corroded Pipes**: Corrosion can lead to pinhole leaks, which may not be immediately visible but can cause extensive damage [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 29].
- **Improper Installation**: Incorrect installation of fixtures can lead to leaks; for example, if the valve stems of a faucet do not match the spacing of the sink holes [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 393].
