## Service Overview

### What is Window Sill Leak Repair?

Window sill leak repair involves addressing issues where water penetrates the sill area of your windows, potentially leading to significant damage over time. During the repair process, our team will assess the condition of your window sills, applying self-adhesive flashing tape to create a watertight seal [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 140]. If necessary, we may replace damaged sill pans or install new flashing to effectively channel water away from the interior [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 141]. The process includes caulking seams and ensuring that all materials used are compatible to maintain a strong defense against Seattle's rainy weather [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 152].
