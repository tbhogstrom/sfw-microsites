## Service Overview

### What is Sliding Glass Door Leak Repair?

Sliding glass door leak repair involves identifying and rectifying issues that allow water to penetrate through the door frame. This service typically includes:

- **Assessment**: Identifying the source of the leak, whether it be faulty seals, damaged frames, or improper installation.
- **Repair Techniques**: Applying weatherstripping, sealing gaps with acrylic latex and silicone caulking, and replacing damaged glass or frames as necessary [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 156].
- **Finalization**: Ensuring all repairs are watertight and the door operates smoothly.

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's unique climate, characterized by significant rainfall and humidity, can exacerbate issues with sliding glass doors. Leaks that may seem minor can lead to mold growth, wood rot, and increased energy bills. Ensuring your sliding glass doors are properly sealed is critical for maintaining your home's comfort, safety, and energy efficiency.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Drafts and Water Infiltration**: Poor sealing can lead to uncomfortable drafts and moisture inside the home [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 135].
- **Damaged Weatherstripping**: Over time, weatherstripping can wear out, allowing leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 140].
- **Failed Sealant**: Aged or improperly applied sealants can crack and fail, contributing to leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 156].
- **Misalignment of Doors**: Doors that are not properly aligned can create gaps, leading to leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 156].
