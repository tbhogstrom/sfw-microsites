## Service Overview

### What is Window Flashing Repair and Installation?

Window flashing is a waterproof barrier installed around windows to prevent water from penetrating the building envelope. Our service involves assessing the existing flashing, repairing any damaged sections, and installing new flashing as needed. We use high-quality materials and techniques to ensure a watertight seal. This includes applying self-adhesive flashing tape, rigid sill pans, and cap flashing to redirect water effectively away from window frames [Source: Litchfield, Page 140; Source: Litchfield, Page 142].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington

Seattle's unique weather patterns, characterized by frequent rains, necessitate a robust flashing system to prevent water intrusion. Many homes in the area are susceptible to water damage due to outdated or improperly installed flashing. Adhering to local building codes and using appropriate materials is crucial in safeguarding your home from moisture-related issues, which can lead to expensive repairs over time.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Water Leaks**: Without proper flashing, water can seep into the home, causing damage to walls and windows [Source: Litchfield, Page 140].
- **Mold Growth**: Moisture intrusion can result in mold and mildew, presenting health risks to occupants [Source: Litchfield, Page 148].
- **Rotting Wood**: Improperly flashed windows can lead to wood rot, compromising structural integrity [Source: Litchfield, Page 141].
- **Energy Inefficiency**: Gaps in window flashing can lead to increased energy bills due to drafts and heat loss [Source: Litchfield, Page 166].
