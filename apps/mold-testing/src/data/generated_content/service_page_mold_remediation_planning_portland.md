## Service Overview

### What is Mold Remediation Planning?

Mold remediation planning involves a systematic approach to identify, contain, and eliminate mold from residential or commercial properties. This process includes an initial assessment to gauge the extent of mold growth and moisture intrusion, followed by the development of a tailored remediation plan. Professionals utilize protective gear, such as N95 respirators and disposable coveralls, during the remediation to ensure safety while containing mold spores and debris [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 415].

The planning phase also addresses necessary materials and techniques for effective mold removal, ensuring all affected areas are sealed off to prevent the spread of spores during the process. Key steps include utilizing commercial-grade HEPA vacuums and scrubbing surfaces with soapy water to remove visible mold [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 455].
