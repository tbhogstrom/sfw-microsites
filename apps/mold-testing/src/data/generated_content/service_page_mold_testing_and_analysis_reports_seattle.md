## Service Overview

### What is Mold Testing and Analysis Reports?

Mold testing involves the collection and analysis of air and surface samples to identify the type and concentration of mold spores present in your environment. Our process includes visual inspections, air quality testing, and laboratory analysis of samples. 

As referenced in construction guidelines, effective mold remediation necessitates first understanding the extent of the mold infestation. This is critical for determining the appropriate remediation strategies [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 415]. Our thorough assessment helps in identifying not just visible mold but also hidden mold that may be present within the walls or under flooring.
