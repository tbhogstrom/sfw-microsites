## Service Overview

### What is Post-Remediation Mold Testing?

Post-remediation mold testing involves a systematic evaluation of the affected areas after a mold remediation project. This process includes visual inspections, air sampling, and surface testing to confirm that mold spores are at acceptable levels. Following industry standards, our team utilizes commercial-grade HEPA vacuums and moisture meters to ensure that all surfaces are free from contaminants [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 415]. 

Sealing off affected areas with plastic sheeting and turning off HVAC systems during testing prevents cross-contamination, ensuring accurate results [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 414]. This meticulous approach allows us to determine if the remediation was successful and if further action is needed.

## Our Process

### Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Initial Assessment**: Our certified professionals conduct a thorough inspection of the previously affected areas, documenting any visible signs of mold and moisture.

2. **Planning & Preparation**: We prepare for testing by sealing off the work area and ensuring that all necessary equipment—like moisture meters and HEPA vacuums—is on site.

3. **Execution**: During testing, we collect air samples and surface samples from suspected areas, utilizing methods that comply with EPA standards to ensure accuracy [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 415].

4. **Quality Check & Cleanup**: After testing, we review the results and ensure that any additional remediation is conducted if necessary. We also clean up the work area, safely disposing of any contaminated materials [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 415].

5. **Follow-up**: We provide you with a detailed report of our findings and recommendations for maintaining a mold-free environment.
