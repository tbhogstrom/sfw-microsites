## Our Process

### Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Initial Assessment**: Our team conducts a thorough visual inspection of your property, looking for signs of mold, moisture, and water damage in key areas such as bathrooms, kitchens, and basements.

2. **Planning & Preparation**: Before starting the inspection, we prepare by discussing potential concerns with you, understanding your property’s history, and ensuring all necessary permits and safety measures are in place.

3. **Execution**: We utilize specialized tools like moisture meters and infrared cameras to assess moisture levels and identify hidden mold. For safety, all affected areas are sealed, and any cutting of contaminated materials is done with a HEPA vacuum nearby to minimize dust spread [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 415].

4. **Quality Check & Cleanup**: After the inspection, we ensure that all equipment is cleaned and the work area is left tidy. Our team will also explain our findings in detail, ensuring you understand the situation.

5. **Follow-up**: We provide a detailed report of our findings, including recommendations for remediation and prevention strategies to keep your home mold-free.
