## Service Overview

### What is Mold Prevention Education?

Mold Prevention Education involves comprehensive training designed to help homeowners understand mold growth, its causes, and effective prevention strategies. This service includes an overview of the types of mold, how to identify moisture-prone areas, and the importance of maintaining proper humidity levels. Techniques such as using moisture meters to check framing moisture content (ideally 15% to 20% or less) and sealing doorways and heating registers during remediation are emphasized to contain mold spores effectively [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 415].
