## Service Overview

### What is Custom Metal Flashing Fabrication?

Custom metal flashing fabrication involves creating bespoke metal pieces that protect critical joints and seams on your structure from water infiltration. This service includes the fabrication of various types of flashing, such as drip-edge flashing, chimney flashing, vent-pipe flashing, and step flashing. Using materials such as copper, galvanized steel, or aluminum, our skilled craftsmen ensure that each piece fits perfectly and is installed with precision to maximize its effectiveness. 

Notably, the installation of flashing requires a careful approach; nails should be placed strategically to avoid water pooling, and overlapping sections must be executed correctly to prevent leaks [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 94].
