## Service Overview

### What is Skylight Flashing Installation?

Skylight flashing installation involves the strategic placement of metal or waterproof materials around the skylight to redirect water away from the opening. This process includes installing apron flashing, step-flashing, and head flashing to ensure a watertight seal. The installation must align with the roofing materials and local building codes. For effective installation, the apron flashing should snugly fit against the skylight curb, and the step flashing must overlap the shingles appropriately to prevent water from seeping underneath [Source: Litchfield, Page 162].
