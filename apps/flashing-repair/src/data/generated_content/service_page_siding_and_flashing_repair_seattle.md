## Service Overview

### What is Siding and Flashing Repair?

Siding and flashing repair involves the maintenance and restoration of the materials that protect your home from water infiltration and other environmental factors. Flashings are critical components that should tuck under materials above them and overlap those below, ensuring that water is directed away from the building structure [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 184]. This process includes assessing the integrity of existing siding, replacing damaged sections, and ensuring that all flashings are properly installed and sealed.
