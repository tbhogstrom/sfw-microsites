## Service Overview

### What is Step Flashing Repair?

Step flashing repair involves fixing or replacing the metal flashing installed at the junctions of a roof and vertical surfaces, such as walls or chimneys. It consists of L-shaped pieces that are layered and overlapped to create a watertight seal. Proper installation requires that the flashing be aligned with the top of the shingles and secured in a way that allows for water drainage without obstruction [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 192]. 

Common issues include improperly sized or positioned flashing, which can lead to gaps where water can enter the home [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 192]. Our skilled technicians will assess your existing flashing and make necessary repairs or replacements to ensure optimal protection.
