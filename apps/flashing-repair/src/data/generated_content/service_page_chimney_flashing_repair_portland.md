## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Flashing Repair?

Chimney flashing repair involves the replacement or restoration of the metal flashing that seals the junction between the chimney and the roof. This service is essential for maintaining the waterproof integrity of your roofing system. During the repair process, we typically replace old or damaged flashing with durable materials, often opting for copper over aluminum due to its superior longevity and resistance to rust [Source: (For Pros By Pros), Page 180]. Proper flashing installation includes a combination of step flashing and counterflashing, which are designed to redirect water away from the vulnerable areas of your roof [Source: (For Pros By Pros), Page 181].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's climate is characterized by significant rainfall and moisture, making proper chimney flashing essential for homeowners. The prevalence of leaks can lead to rot in the roofing structure, which in turn necessitates costly repairs [Source: (For Pros By Pros), Page 113]. Additionally, local building codes require proper flashing installation to ensure safety and compliance. Failing to address flashing issues can lead to mold growth and hazardous living conditions, necessitating immediate attention.
