## Service Overview

### What is Skylight Flashing Replacement?

Skylight flashing replacement involves the removal of old, damaged, or ineffective flashing materials and replacing them with new, high-quality options designed to prevent water infiltration. This process includes installing step-flashing, apron flashing, and head flashing, as outlined in the industry standards. The flashing should overlap the shingles and extend high enough to be protected from rain, ensuring a watertight seal [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 162].

Proper installation requires careful attention to detail, including the use of caulk beneath exposed edges and gasketed roofing nails to secure the flashing in place. Failing to install flashing correctly can lead to significant water damage, necessitating costly repairs down the line.
