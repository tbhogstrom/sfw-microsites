## Service Overview

### What is Wall Flashing Repair?

Wall flashing repair involves identifying and fixing issues related to the flashing that protects your home from water intrusion. Flashing is typically made from metal, such as aluminum or copper, and is installed at joints, edges, and transitions in roofing and siding. This service includes inspecting the integrity of existing flashing, making repairs or replacements as necessary, and ensuring that all flashing directs water away from critical areas.

Effective flashing should tuck under roofing materials above and overlap with the materials below, guiding water away from seams and preventing it from entering the home [Source: Christina Glennon - Siding, Roofing, and Trim, Page 184]. Proper installation practices, including the use of backer rods and sealants, are crucial to ensure a watertight seal [Source: Charlie Wing - The Visual Handbook of Building and Remodeling, Page 77].
