## Service Overview

### What is Wall and Roof Junction Flashing Services?

Wall and Roof Junction Flashing Services involve the installation of protective barriers at critical points where walls meet roofs, specifically designed to channel water away from these junctions. This process includes the application of step flashing, counterflashing, and other specialized materials to ensure maximum protection. According to industry standards, flashing should tuck under what is above it and over what is below it, directing water toward the exterior effectively [Source: Glennon, Page 184]. 

Our services include an initial assessment of existing flashing systems, the installation of new materials, and repair of any compromised areas. We follow best practices to ensure that flashing is installed correctly, preventing potential leaks caused by improper techniques such as lazy nailing or misaligned materials [Source: Glennon, Page 189].
