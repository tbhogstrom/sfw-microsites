## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Flashing Repair?

Chimney flashing repair involves the assessment and restoration of the waterproof barriers around your chimney. This process typically includes replacing damaged or corroded flashing, installing new counterflashing, and ensuring a watertight seal. Copper flashing is often recommended due to its superior durability compared to aluminum, which can degrade over time [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 180]. Proper installation techniques, such as using a roofing membrane and ensuring a snug fit, are crucial for effective repair [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 113].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington

Seattle's rainy climate emphasizes the need for effective chimney flashing. Water can easily infiltrate through poorly installed flashing, leading to extensive damage. The presence of moss and other growths on roofs in the Pacific Northwest can further exacerbate wear and tear on flashing materials [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 98]. Moreover, local building codes require robust waterproofing measures to protect homes from moisture intrusion.
