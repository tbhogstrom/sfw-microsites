### What are Custom Metal Flashing Solutions?

Custom metal flashing involves the installation of metal barriers designed to protect your home from water infiltration at critical junctions, such as around chimneys, vents, and roof edges. These solutions are tailored to fit your home's specifications and are made from durable materials such as copper, aluminum, or galvanized steel, which resist corrosion and wear [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 94].

Proper flashing installation is vital; it must be positioned correctly to divert water away from seams and to avoid common pitfalls such as misplaced step flashing or improperly sealed joints.
