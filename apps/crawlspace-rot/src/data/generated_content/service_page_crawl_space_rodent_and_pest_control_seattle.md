## Our Process

### Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Initial Assessment**: Our team conducts a thorough inspection of your crawl space, identifying signs of rodent activity, moisture issues, and potential entry points. We utilize moisture meters and visual inspections to gauge the condition of the area.

2. **Planning & Preparation**: Based on our findings, we develop a tailored pest control plan that includes sealing entry points, enhancing ventilation, and improving moisture barriers. This may involve obtaining necessary permits, especially if structural modifications are required.

3. **Execution**: We implement our pest control strategies, which may include the installation of heavy-duty plastic sheeting as a moisture barrier, sealing gaps with high-quality caulk, and employing traps or bait stations for rodent control. Proper sealing techniques will prevent moisture accumulation, a key factor in pest attraction [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 414].

4. **Quality Check & Cleanup**: After executing our plan, we perform a thorough inspection to ensure all measures are effective. We also clean up any debris or waste left from the infestation, maintaining a safe and sanitary environment.

5. **Follow-up**: We schedule follow-up visits to monitor the situation, ensuring that our pest control measures remain effective and making adjustments as necessary.
