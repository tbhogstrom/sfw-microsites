## Service Overview

### What is Crawlspace Ventilation Improvement?

Crawlspace ventilation improvement involves a series of strategic measures designed to enhance air circulation in your crawlspace. This process may include the installation of vents, sealing of gaps, and the application of vapor barriers to prevent moisture buildup. According to building codes, unconditioned crawlspaces must have sufficient ventilation to reduce humidity levels and prevent mold growth. The International Residential Code (IRC) requires proper vent sizing to ensure adequate airflow, typically needing a minimum vent area of 1 square foot for every 150 square feet of crawlspace floor area [Source: Charlie Wing, Page 108].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's climate is characterized by high humidity and frequent rainfall, making crawlspace ventilation particularly vital.
