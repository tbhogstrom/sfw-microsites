## Crawlspace Access Door Installation
Installing a crawlspace access door is an essential step in maintaining a safe and functional crawlspace in Seattle's damp climate. Proper installation begins with selecting a durable door, capable of withstanding the Pacific Northwest's heavy rain and moisture. A prehung door can be an excellent choice, thanks to its built-in frame which helps ensure a snug fit and reduces the risk of water intrusion<sup>1</sup>. Begin by measuring the rough opening and ensuring the door frame aligns flush with the surrounding surfaces. Utilize shims to make sure the jamb is plumb, as proper alignment is crucial to preventing drafts and moisture penetration<sup>2</sup>. Once the door is aligned, secure it with nails, ensuring the threshold is clear of the floor to accommodate any necessary door sweeps or shoes for added insulation and protection. Installing a crawlspace door correctly not only helps maintain a dry and energy-efficient home but also provides convenient access for maintenance and inspections.
