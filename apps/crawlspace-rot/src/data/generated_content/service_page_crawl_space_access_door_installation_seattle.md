## Service Overview

### What is Crawl Space Access Door Installation?

Crawl space access door installation involves creating an entry point into the crawl space beneath your home. This service includes selecting appropriate door materials, ensuring proper fit, and sealing to prevent moisture intrusion. The installation process typically involves measuring the crawl space opening, cutting the access point if necessary, and fitting the door securely. The installation must comply with local building codes to ensure safety and effectiveness [Source: Litchfield, 2013, Page 119].
