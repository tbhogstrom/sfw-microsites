## Service Overview

### What is Crawl Space Water Drainage Solutions?

Crawl space water drainage solutions involve the installation of systems designed to divert water away from your home's foundation. This typically includes the use of sump pumps, drainage channels, and vapor barriers. Our process begins with a thorough inspection of your crawl space to identify existing moisture issues. We then implement a tailored drainage plan that may include:

- Installing **perforated drain pipes** to collect and redirect groundwater [Source: Charlie Wing, Page 97].
- Implementing **vapor barriers** to prevent moisture from evaporating into the crawl space [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 414].
- Utilizing **sump pumps** to actively remove water from the crawl space, ensuring it remains dry [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 245].
