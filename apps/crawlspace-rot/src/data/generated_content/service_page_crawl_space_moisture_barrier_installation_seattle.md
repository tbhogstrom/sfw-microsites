## Service Overview

### What is Crawl Space Moisture Barrier Installation?

Crawl space moisture barrier installation involves the application of a vapor retarder over the soil in your crawl space. This retarder serves as a barrier against moisture that can cause damage to your home. The process includes:

1. **Assessment of the Crawl Space**: Evaluating the current condition and moisture levels.
2. **Preparation**: Cleaning the area and repairing any existing damage.
3. **Installation**: Laying down the vapor barrier, ensuring it is sealed properly to prevent any moisture infiltration.
4. **Quality Checks**: Ensuring that all installations meet local building codes.

## Our Process

### Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Initial Assessment**: Our team will conduct a thorough inspection of your crawl space to identify moisture levels and any existing issues. This step is crucial to tailor our approach effectively.

2. **Planning & Preparation**: We will prepare the area by cleaning it and addressing any damages. This may include repairing insulation and ensuring proper drainage systems are in place. We will also obtain any necessary permits to conform to local regulations.

3. **Execution**: The installation of the moisture barrier involves laying a high-quality vapor retarder over the soil surface, ensuring that it overlaps and is sealed at the seams with compatible tape.
