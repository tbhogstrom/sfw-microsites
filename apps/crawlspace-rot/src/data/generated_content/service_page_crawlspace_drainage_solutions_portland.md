## Service Overview

### What is Crawlspace Drainage Solutions?

Crawlspace drainage solutions refer to a variety of methods and systems designed to prevent excess moisture accumulation in your crawlspace. This process typically involves:

- **Assessing and Improving Site Grading**: Ensuring the ground slopes away from your home to direct water away from the foundation.
- **Installing Drainage Systems**: Incorporating footing drains, sump pumps, and drainage channels that collect and redirect water away from the crawlspace [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 414].
- **Implementing Vapor Barriers**: Laying heavy-duty plastic sheeting over the crawlspace floor to prevent moisture from rising up through the soil [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 414].
- **Enhancing Ventilation**: Improving airflow to lower humidity levels, which discourages mold growth [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 414].
