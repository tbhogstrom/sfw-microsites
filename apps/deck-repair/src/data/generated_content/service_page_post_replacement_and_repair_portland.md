## 2. Service Overview
### What is Post Replacement and Repair?

Post replacement and repair involves the assessment and restoration of deck posts that have suffered from damage or deterioration, often caused by moisture, insects, or structural issues. This service is vital for maintaining the structural integrity of decks, porches, and other outdoor structures.

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland, where the climate can be damp and rainy, wooden posts are particularly susceptible to rot and decay. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can prevent small issues from turning into costly repairs. By investing in post replacement and repair, homeowners can:

- Maintain safety for occupants and visitors.
- Preserve the aesthetic appeal of their outdoor spaces.
- Avoid more extensive and expensive repairs down the line.

### Common Problems This Service Solves

1. **Rotting Wood**: Posts that have absorbed moisture can rot, leading to structural failure [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 253].
2. **Insect Damage**: Termites and other pests can compromise the integrity of wooden posts.
3. **Incorrect Installation**: Posts not securely anchored or installed can lead to instability [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook, Page 639].
4. **Weather Effects**: Portland's damp climate can exacerbate wear and tear, making timely repairs essential.
