## 7. FAQ Section

### 1. How often should I refinish my deck?
It's recommended to refinish your deck every 1-3 years, depending on the wear and exposure to elements.

### 2. What materials do you use for refinishing?
We use high-quality, eco-friendly stains and sealants specifically designed for Seattle's climate.

### 3. Will refinishing my deck prevent mold and moss?
Yes, regular refinishing helps prevent mold and moss growth by protecting the wood from moisture [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 541].

### 4. Do I need to be home during the refinishing?
While it's not necessary, we recommend being available to discuss any questions or preferences during the process.

### 5. Can you repair damaged boards during refinishing?
Absolutely! We assess the deck for any damage and can replace or repair boards as needed.

### 6. How long does the refinishing process take?
Typically, refinishing a deck takes 1-3 days, depending on the size and condition of the deck.

### 7. What weather conditions are suitable for refinishing?
It's best to refinish your deck when temperatures are between 50°F and 90°F with low humidity to ensure optimal drying conditions.

### 8. Is your process eco-friendly?
Yes, we prioritize using low-VOC and eco-friendly products to protect the environment.

### 9. How can I prepare my deck for refinishing?
Ensure the deck is clear of furniture, pots, and other items. We will handle the rest, including cleaning and preparing the surface.

### 10. What should I do after my deck is refinished?
You should avoid heavy foot traffic for at least 24-48 hours after refinishing to allow the finish to cure properly.
