## Deck Skirting or Lattice Repair
Deck skirting and lattice are essential features for many Seattle homes, offering both aesthetic appeal and functional benefits by concealing unsightly structural elements and providing a barrier against pests. However, the Pacific Northwest's notorious rain and moisture can take a toll, leading to rot and damage over time. Repairing deck skirting or lattice involves replacing damaged sections with new materials that can withstand the damp climate. Pressure-treated wood is a popular choice due to its resistance to decay when exposed to moisture<sup>1</sup>. When repairing, it's crucial to ensure proper installation techniques are followed, such as using galvanized fasteners to prevent corrosion and ensuring adequate ventilation to reduce moisture buildup<sup>2</sup>. By addressing these common issues, homeowners can protect their decks from further damage and maintain their home's overall aesthetic.
