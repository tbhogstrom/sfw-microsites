## Service Overview

### What is Deck Drainage Solutions?

**Deck Drainage Solutions** encompass a range of techniques and systems designed to manage water runoff and moisture around and beneath your deck. By implementing effective drainage solutions, we protect your deck structure from water damage, prolonging its lifespan and ensuring a safe outdoor environment.

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington

Seattle's unique climate, characterized by frequent rainfall and high humidity, makes effective deck drainage essential. Water accumulation can lead to structural issues, including wood rot, mold growth, and decreased aesthetics. By investing in professional deck drainage solutions, homeowners can prevent costly repairs and maintain their outdoor living spaces.

### Common Problems This Service Solves

1. **Water Accumulation:** Excess water pooling around or under the deck.
2. **Mold and Mildew Growth:** Increased moisture leads to unhealthy living environments.
3. **Deck Rot:** Wood deterioration due to prolonged exposure to moisture.
4. **Structural Compromise:** Weakening of deck supports from water damage.

Effective drainage systems, such as installing **perforated drainage pipes** and **vapor barriers**, can significantly mitigate these issues [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 245].

## Our Process

### Step-by-Step Breakdown:

1. **Initial Assessment:**
   We begin with a thorough examination of your deck and surrounding area, identifying potential drainage issues. This includes evaluating the slope and soil permeability of your property [Source: Charlie Wing, The Visual Handbook, Page 107].

2. **Planning & Preparation:**
   Based on our assessment, we design a customized drainage plan tailored to your deck's specific needs. This may involve selecting the right materials, such as **rigid PVC discharge pipes** or **synthetic rubber membranes**, to ensure durability and effectiveness [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 269].

3. **Execution:**
   Our experienced team will implement the drainage system, ensuring proper installation of all components. This includes placing **perforated pipes** beneath the deck to redirect water away from critical areas, as well as ensuring a **20-foot discharge distance** from the house [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 268].

4. **Quality Check & Cleanup:**
   After installation, we conduct a detailed inspection to ensure everything is functioning correctly. We also clean up the work area, leaving your property as pristine as we found it.

5. **Follow-up:**
   To ensure long-term satisfaction, we offer follow-up services to inspect the drainage system and make any necessary adjustments.
