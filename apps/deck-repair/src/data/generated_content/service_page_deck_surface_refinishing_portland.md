## FAQ Section
### 1. How often should I refinish my deck?
Generally, decks should be refinished every 1-3 years, depending on exposure to weather conditions and the type of finish used.

### 2. What signs indicate that my deck needs refinishing?
Look for fading color, rough texture, peeling paint, or mold and mildew growth, which are all signs that refinishing is needed.

### 3. Can I refinish my deck myself?
While DIY refinishing is possible, professional services ensure high-quality results, especially in preparing and treating the wood properly.

### 4. What types of finishes do you use?
We offer a range of finishes including oil-based stains, water-based sealants, and low-VOC options to meet environmental standards [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 540].

### 5. Will refinishing my deck prevent mold growth?
Yes, refinishing with the right sealants can help repel moisture and prevent mold and mildew growth, especially in Portland’s humid climate [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 541].

### 6. How do you prepare the deck for refinishing?
Preparation includes cleaning, sanding, and inspecting for damage to ensure a smooth application of the new finish.

### 7. What if my deck has severe damage?
In cases of severe damage, we recommend repair or replacement of affected boards before refinishing.

### 8. How long does the refinishing process take?
Most refinishing jobs can be completed within 1-3 days, depending on the size of the deck and the weather conditions.

### 9. Are your products environmentally friendly?
Yes, we prioritize using eco-friendly products that are low in VOCs and safe for your family and pets.

### 10. What maintenance does my deck need after refinishing?
Regular cleaning and inspection for damage will help maintain your deck’s appearance and longevity.
