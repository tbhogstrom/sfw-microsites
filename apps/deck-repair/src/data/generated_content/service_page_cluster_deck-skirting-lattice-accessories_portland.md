## Deck Skirting or Lattice Repair
Deck skirting and lattice work not only elevate the aesthetic appeal of your deck but also serve practical purposes like providing storage space and keeping pests at bay. However, the Pacific Northwest's rainy climate can lead to moisture-related issues such as rot or warping, especially in older homes. It's crucial to address these problems promptly to maintain structural integrity. When repairing deck skirting or lattice, our approach involves assessing the extent of the damage and replacing only the affected portions with durable materials like pressure-treated wood or composite alternatives. This method preserves the overall design while ensuring longevity<sup>1</sup>. For lattice, using high-strength epoxy at the joints can reinforce the structure and prevent future separations<sup>2</sup>. If your deck skirting or lattice shows signs of wear, contact us to schedule a thorough inspection and keep your deck in top shape for years to come.
