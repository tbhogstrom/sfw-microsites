## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Flashing Repair?

Chimney flashing repair involves inspecting, replacing, or repairing the metal flashing that seals the area where your chimney meets the roof. This service is vital because chimney flashing is often a common source of leaks. A thorough inspection typically reveals the condition of the existing flashing and whether it needs replacement or repair. 

The repair process may include:
- Removing old, damaged flashing.
- Installing new step and counter-flashing to ensure water is directed away from the chimney [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 181].
