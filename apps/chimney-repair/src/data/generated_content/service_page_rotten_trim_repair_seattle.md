## 2. Service Overview

### What is Rotten Trim Repair?

Rotten Trim Repair involves the careful restoration or replacement of deteriorating trim around windows, doors, and other exterior structures. This service is essential for maintaining a home’s appearance and preventing further damage caused by moisture and pests.

### Why is it Important for Homeowners in Seattle, Washington?

Seattle’s unique climate, characterized by high humidity and frequent rainfall, creates the perfect conditions for wood rot. Homeowners need to be vigilant about maintaining their trim to prevent extensive damage. If left untreated, rotten trim can lead to structural issues, increased energy costs, and the potential for mold growth within walls.

### Common Problems This Service Solves

- **Visible Wood Rot:** Signs of decay can manifest as soft, crumbly wood, stains, and peeling paint [Source: Glennon, Page 6].
- **Water Damage:** Poorly maintained trim can allow moisture to seep into the home, leading to mold and mildew growth [Source: Litchfield, Page 173].
- **Pest Infestations:** Rotting wood can attract pests like termites, which can cause further damage [Source: Litchfield, Page 55].

By addressing these issues promptly, homeowners can safeguard their property and maintain its value.

## 7. FAQ Section

### 1. What causes wood trim to rot?
Wood trim rots primarily due to prolonged exposure to moisture, poor drainage, or inadequate sealing and painting.

### 2. How can I tell if my trim needs repair or replacement?
Signs include soft spots, discoloration, peeling paint, and visible cracks. A professional assessment can provide clarity [Source: Litchfield, Page 207].

### 3. What materials do you use for repairs?
We typically use high-strength marine epoxy for repairs and high-quality wood or PVC trim for replacements [Source: Glennon, Page 198].

### 4. How long does the repair process take?
Depending on the extent of the damage, repairs can take anywhere from a few hours to a couple of days.

### 5. Will I need to repaint my trim after repairs?
Yes, we recommend repainting to protect the new trim and ensure a uniform appearance.

### 6. Can you match my existing trim?
Yes, we can replicate existing profiles to maintain your home’s character [Source: Litchfield, Page 21].

### 7. Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes, we stand behind our work and offer warranties on repairs and installations.

### 8. How can I prevent my trim from rotting in the future?
Regular maintenance, including painting, sealing, and ensuring proper drainage, can significantly reduce the risk of rot [Source: Glennon, Page 6].
