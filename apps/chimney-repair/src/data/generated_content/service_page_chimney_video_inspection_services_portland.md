## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Video Inspection Services?

Chimney video inspection services involve inserting a high-definition camera into the chimney flue to capture real-time footage of its interior condition. This allows our trained technicians to evaluate critical elements, such as the integrity of flue tiles, the presence of creosote buildup, and any cracks or structural damage in the chimney. According to the **Chimney Safety Institute of America**, it is essential to conduct these inspections annually to maintain safety and compliance with local building codes [Source: CSIA].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's unique climate, characterized by significant rainfall and humidity, can lead to accelerated deterioration of chimney structures. The moisture can foster mold growth and promote corrosion in metal components, making regular inspections critical for homeowners. Additionally, local building codes mandate inspections for certain types of chimney installations, emphasizing the need for professional services to ensure compliance and safety.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Creosote Buildup**: A sticky, flammable substance that can accumulate in the chimney if not cleaned regularly, leading to potential chimney fires [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 235].
- **Cracked Flue Tiles**: Damaged flue tiles can allow superheated gases to escape, creating a fire hazard and compromising the chimney’s efficiency [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 236].
- **Deteriorating Mortar Joints**: Crumbling mortar can weaken the chimney structure, potentially leading to complete failure if not addressed [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 21].
- **Obstructions**: Debris, nests, or other blockages can restrict airflow, increasing the risk of smoke and gas backdrafts [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 236].
