## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Flashing Repair and Waterproofing?

Chimney flashing repair and waterproofing involve the inspection, replacement, and sealing of the flashing around your chimney to create a watertight barrier. This service typically includes the following steps:

1. **Assessment of Existing Flashing**: We begin by evaluating the condition of your chimney flashing to identify any weaknesses or failures.
2. **Replacement of Damaged Materials**: If the existing aluminum flashing is corroded or damaged, we recommend replacing it with superior materials like copper, which lasts longer and resists deterioration [Source: Reference Material, Page 180].
3. **Installation of New Flashing**: New flashing will be custom-fitted to your chimney, including step flashing and counterflashing to ensure proper water diversion.
4. **Waterproofing Measures**: Additional waterproofing techniques, such as underlayment installation, will be applied to further enhance protection against water intrusion [Source: Reference Material, Page 181].
