## Service Overview

### What is Wood Chimney Chase Framing Repair?

Wood chimney chase framing repair involves the inspection, reinforcement, and replacement of the structural elements surrounding your chimney. This service typically includes repairing or replacing damaged wood framing, addressing any moisture intrusion, and ensuring the chimney is adequately insulated and sealed. Common techniques involve using durable materials that withstand Portland's variable climate, from excessive rain to seasonal winds.
