## Service Overview

### What is Chimney Rebuilding Services?

Chimney rebuilding involves extensive repair and reconstruction of a chimney that has suffered damage due to age, weather exposure, or improper construction. Our services include:

1. **Assessment and Inspection**: Our technicians conduct a thorough inspection of your chimney to identify issues such as cracked bricks, deteriorated mortar, and missing flue liners. [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 21]
  
2. **Mortar Repointing**: If the mortar joints are crumbling, repointing may be necessary. This involves removing old mortar and replacing it with fresh mortar to restore structural integrity. [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 236]

3. **Flue Lining Installation**: If your chimney lacks a flue-tile lining or has damaged liners, we can install a poured masonry liner or a stainless-steel flue, ensuring safe venting of gases. [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 236]

4. **Crown Replacement**: We can replace or repair your chimney crown, which is essential for directing water away and preventing leaks. [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 236]
