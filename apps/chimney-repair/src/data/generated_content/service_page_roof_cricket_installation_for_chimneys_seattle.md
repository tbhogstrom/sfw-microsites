### What is Roof Cricket Installation for Chimneys?

Roof cricket installation involves creating a sloped structure made of durable materials that diverts water and snow away from the chimney. This includes fabricating the cricket from heavy-gauge galvanized steel or other suitable materials, ensuring it is properly integrated with existing roofing and flashing systems. As noted in various construction resources, the cricket must be designed to overlap with step-flashing and counter-flashing components to create a watertight seal [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 98]. 

The installation process requires precise measurements and skilled workmanship to ensure that the cricket effectively channels water away from the chimney, thus preventing pooling and leaks which can be detrimental in Seattle's wet climate.
