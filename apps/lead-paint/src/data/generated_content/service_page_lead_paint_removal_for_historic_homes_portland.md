## Service Overview

### What is Lead Paint Removal for Historic Homes?

Lead paint removal involves the careful and safe extraction of lead-based paint from surfaces in your home. This process includes identifying areas with lead paint, applying appropriate containment measures, and using specialized techniques to safely remove the paint without contaminating the surrounding environment. Techniques may include hand-scraping, chemical strippers, or heat removal methods, all executed with strict adherence to safety protocols [Source: Michael Litchfield, Renovation, Page 525].
