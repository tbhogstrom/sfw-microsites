## Service Overview

### What is Chemical Lead Paint Stripping?

Chemical lead paint stripping involves using specially formulated chemical strippers to dissolve and remove layers of lead-based paint from various surfaces. These strippers are applied to painted areas, allowed to work for a specified time, and then gently scraped away, revealing clean, bare surfaces beneath. This process is particularly effective for intricate details and tight spaces where mechanical scrapers may struggle [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 66].

Using chemical strippers is ideal for homes built before 1978, as lead paint can pose significant health risks if disturbed improperly. It’s essential to approach this process with care to prevent lead dust from contaminating the air [Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 524].
