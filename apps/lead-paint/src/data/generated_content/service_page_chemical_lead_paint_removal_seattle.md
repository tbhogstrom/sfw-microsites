## Service Overview

### What is Chemical Lead Paint Removal?

Chemical lead paint removal involves the use of specially formulated chemical strippers to safely and effectively remove lead-based paint from surfaces. These strippers are applied to the painted areas and allowed to penetrate the paint layer. After a specified time, the paint begins to blister, allowing for gentle scraping without damaging the underlying wood [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 66]. This method is particularly effective in intricate areas where mechanical scrapers may not reach and minimizes the risk of lead dust becoming airborne.
