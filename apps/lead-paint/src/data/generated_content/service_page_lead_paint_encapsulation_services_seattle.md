## Service Overview

### What is Lead Paint Encapsulation Services?

Lead Paint Encapsulation is a specialized service that involves applying a protective coating over lead-based paint surfaces to contain and seal in the hazardous material. This process reduces the risk of lead dust becoming airborne, thereby protecting the health of occupants. The encapsulation material is designed to adhere securely to the existing paint, creating a barrier that prevents lead exposure without the need for extensive removal processes [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 66].
