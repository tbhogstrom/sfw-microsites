## Service Overview

### What is Lead Hazard Control Consultation?

A Lead Hazard Control Consultation involves a comprehensive evaluation of your property to identify potential lead hazards, primarily from lead-based paint and dust. Our certified professionals will conduct thorough testing, including paint sampling and dust wipe tests, to assess lead levels in your home. If lead is detected, we will provide a detailed plan for remediation, outlining safe practices and compliance with the EPA's Renovation, Repair, and Painting (RRP) rule, which mandates specific procedures for handling lead in homes built before 1978 [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation-Taunton Press, Page 524].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland has a rich history of housing built before the 1978 lead paint ban, making lead hazard control essential for homeowners. The city's damp climate can exacerbate issues related to lead paint deterioration, leading to increased dust and flaking. Furthermore, Oregon's strict building codes require adherence to safety standards during renovations. By conducting a Lead Hazard Control Consultation, homeowners can ensure compliance with local regulations while protecting their families from lead exposure.
