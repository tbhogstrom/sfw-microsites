## Service Overview

### What is Heat Gun Lead Paint Removal?

Heat gun lead paint removal is a method where electric heat guns are used to soften and lift lead-based paint from surfaces. This technique involves carefully heating the paint to a temperature that allows it to blister and peel away, enabling easy scraping without damaging the substrate.
