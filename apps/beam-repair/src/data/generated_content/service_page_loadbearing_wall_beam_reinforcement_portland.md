## Service Overview

### What is Load-Bearing Wall Beam Reinforcement?

Load-bearing wall beam reinforcement involves the installation of structural beams to support the weight of walls and upper floors. This process is especially important when modifying or removing walls to create open spaces. The reinforcement process typically involves the use of steel I-beams or engineered wood beams, which are integrated into the existing framework to carry the load effectively.

Professional installation is crucial due to the complexity of load distribution, particularly in older homes in Portland that may not have been built to modern standards. The installation requires careful planning, precise measurements, and specialized equipment to ensure safety and compliance with local building codes [Source: Michael Litchfield - Renovation, Page 255].
