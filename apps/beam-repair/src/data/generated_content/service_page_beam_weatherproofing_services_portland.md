## Service Overview

### What is Beam Weatherproofing Services?

Beam weatherproofing involves applying protective membranes and materials to safeguard your structural beams from moisture, rot, and insect damage. This process typically includes the installation of self-adhering waterproof membranes around the beams, sealing joints, and ensuring that any penetrations through the beam structure are adequately flashed. According to industry standards, using products like WSU (Waterproofing Self-Adhering Underlayment) provides a reliable barrier against water intrusion, as it self-seals around nails and other fasteners, ensuring a truly waterproof installation [Source: Litchfield, Page 93].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Portland's unique climate, characterized by high levels of rainfall and humidity, creates an environment where untreated beams are susceptible to water damage and decay. Local building codes emphasize the importance of proper weatherproofing to prevent structural failures. Without adequate protection, homeowners can face severe issues such as mold growth, wood rot, and compromised structural integrity.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Moisture Intrusion**: Excess moisture can lead to rotting beams and structural failure [Source: Litchfield, Page 89].
- **Insect Infestation**: Untreated beams can attract pests, which can compromise the structural integrity [Source: Glennon, Page 140].
- **Inefficient Energy Use**: Poorly sealed beams can lead to drafts and increased energy costs [Source: Litchfield, Page 112].
- **Water Damage**: Inadequate flashing and sealing can lead to leaks, resulting in extensive damage to both beams and surrounding materials [Source: Litchfield, Page 112].
