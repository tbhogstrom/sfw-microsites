## Service Overview

### What is Custom Beam Fabrication?

Custom beam fabrication involves the design, engineering, and construction of beams tailored to meet the specific needs of a structure. This may include glulam beams, LVL (Laminated Veneer Lumber), or steel I-beams, all of which can be customized for size, shape, and material to suit various architectural requirements [Source: Litchfield, Page 75]. The process begins with an assessment of the load requirements and space constraints, followed by the selection of appropriate materials and designs.

In Portland, the local climate can impose additional demands on structural elements, necessitating beams that are not only strong but also resistant to moisture and other weather-related issues.
