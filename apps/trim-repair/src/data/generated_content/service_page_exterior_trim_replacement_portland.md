## Service Overview

### What is Exterior Trim Replacement?

Exterior trim replacement involves removing old, damaged, or rotten trim boards and installing new ones to restore both function and beauty to your home’s exterior. This service typically includes the following steps: assessing the existing trim, selecting appropriate materials, executing a precise replacement, and conducting a thorough quality check to ensure optimal results. Our techniques incorporate industry best practices to ensure durability and visual appeal, including the strategic use of materials like PVC trim for low maintenance and resistance to rot [Source: Glennon, Page 195].

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

In Portland, the unique climate poses specific challenges for exterior trim. With its wet winters and humid summers, wood trim is particularly vulnerable to rot and decay. This makes timely replacement not only a matter of aesthetics but also an essential part of home maintenance. Homeowners must adhere to local building codes that often require durable and rot-resistant materials for long-lasting results. Ensuring your trim is in top shape protects your investment and keeps your home looking great for years to come.

### Common Problems This Service Solves
- **Rotting Trim**: Common in Portland’s wet climate, rotting trim can lead to more severe structural issues if not addressed quickly [Source: Litchfield, Page 173].
- **Aesthetic Decline**: Faded or damaged trim detracts from your home’s overall appearance and may lower property value.
- **Moisture Penetration**: Damaged trim can allow moisture to enter your home, leading to mold and mildew issues [Source: Glennon, Page 197].
- **Improper Installation**: Poorly installed trim can lead to gaps and leaks, making your home susceptible to further damage [Source: Litchfield, Page 488].
