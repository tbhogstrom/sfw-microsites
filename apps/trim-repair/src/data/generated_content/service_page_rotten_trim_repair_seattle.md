## Service Overview

### What is Rotten Trim Repair?

Rotten trim repair involves the process of assessing, repairing, and restoring damaged trim caused by moisture, rot, and other environmental factors. This service includes cutting back to sound wood, reinforcing soft, punky areas with high-strength epoxy, and replacing sections that are beyond repair. Commonly, a skilled professional will utilize techniques such as scarf joints or butt joints, which ensure a secure and aesthetically pleasing finish ([Source: Christina Glennon, Page 196][Source: Michael Litchfield, Page 173]).
