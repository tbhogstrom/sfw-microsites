## Service Overview

### What is Fascia Board Replacement?

Fascia board replacement involves the removal of damaged or rotting fascia and the installation of new boards to restore the protective barrier along the roofline. This task requires careful assessment and execution, ensuring that the new fascia is securely attached and adequately sealed to prevent further moisture issues. The process typically includes cutting back to sound wood, applying high-strength marine epoxy for durability, and ensuring the joints are watertight [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 196]. 

### Why It's Important for Homeowners in Portland, Oregon

Given Portland's rainy climate, fascia boards are particularly vulnerable to rot and decay. The constant moisture can exacerbate existing damage, leading to more severe structural issues if not addressed promptly. Moreover, Portland's building codes mandate that all exterior components, including fascia, meet specific standards to ensure safety and longevity. Regular inspection and timely replacement are essential to comply with local regulations.
