## Service Overview

### What is PVC Trim Installation?

PVC trim installation involves the application of high-quality polyvinyl chloride (PVC) boards to enhance the exterior of your home. This process includes selecting the right profiles, cutting the trim to precise lengths, and installing it according to best practices to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal. The installation process requires specific tools and techniques to accommodate the unique properties of PVC, such as its tendency to expand and contract with temperature changes [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 200].

Careful handling, cutting with carbide-edged tools, and using appropriate fasteners like 16-gauge finish nails and screws are crucial for a successful installation. By following these techniques, we ensure that your PVC trim remains securely in place and looks great throughout its lifespan [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 201].
