## Service Overview

### What is Trim Restoration and Refinishing?

Trim restoration and refinishing involve repairing damaged trim components, such as casing, fascia, and corner boards, rather than replacing them entirely. This process includes identifying rot or damage, treating affected areas with specialized materials like high-strength marine epoxy, and refinishing surfaces to restore their original beauty [Source: For Pros By Pros, Page 197]. 

Our team carefully assesses each trim piece, ensuring that repairs are both aesthetically pleasing and structurally sound. Techniques such as scarf joints and the application of durable finishes are employed to provide long-lasting results [Source: For Pros By Pros, Page 196].
