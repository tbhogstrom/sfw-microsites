### What is Trim Caulking and Sealing?

Trim caulking and sealing involves the application of specialized materials to fill gaps and seams in your trim, siding, and other exterior elements of your home. This process is crucial for creating a barrier against moisture and air infiltration, ensuring the longevity of your home’s exterior. 

Using a combination of high-strength marine epoxy for repairs and effective caulking techniques, our team first assesses the areas that need attention. We utilize foam backer rods to fill deep gaps, ensuring that caulking adheres properly and cures fully, thus providing a smooth and durable seal [Source: Christina Glennon, Page 70].
