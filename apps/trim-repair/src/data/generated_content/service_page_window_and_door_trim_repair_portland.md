## Service Overview

### What is Window and Door Trim Repair?

Window and door trim repair involves assessing and fixing deteriorated or damaged trim around windows and doors. This service includes repairing rotten wood, replacing missing or damaged pieces, and refinishing trim to ensure it looks appealing and functions correctly. Our professional team follows a detailed process, from initial assessment to execution, ensuring that your trim is not only aesthetically pleasing but also structurally sound [Source: Glennon, Page 191].

- **Rotting Trim**: Moisture can lead to wood rot, which compromises the integrity of your trim and can spread to other areas of your home [Source: Glennon, Page 191].
- **Drafts and Water Intrusion**: Poorly fitted or damaged trim can allow drafts and water to penetrate, leading to increased heating costs and potential interior damage [Source: Glennon, Page 210].
- **Aesthetic Mismatches**: Older trim may not match newer windows, creating an inconsistent look that can detract from your home's appeal [Source: Glennon, Page 201].
- **Paint and Finish Damage**: Peeling paint or damaged finishes not only look unattractive but can also expose trim to further weather-related damage [Source: Glennon, Page 195].
